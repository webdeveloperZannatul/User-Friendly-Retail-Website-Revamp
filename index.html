Need help finding the model?

We only need two key details

To help match the correct remote, please provide:

1. Brand

For example: Samsung, Sony, LG, Panasonic, Fujitsu, Daikin, Mitsubishi Electric.

2. Model number

This can often be found on the TV sticker, the air conditioner unit label, or printed on the original remote.

For TVs

Check the back of the television or the original remote body.

For air conditioners

Look on the indoor unit sticker or the original remote label.
